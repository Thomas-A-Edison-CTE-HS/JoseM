In this game of, "Spot the Difference" there are 3 levels you can choose from depending on how difficult you'd like to play the game.

These 3 levels are:

  1. Easy
  2. Medium
  3. Hard

Throughout each level, you will be able to see multiple objects, you only have a few seconds to look at each of them. After that, BOOM! Your screen turns black and turns back on shortly after.

Only 3 of all the objects have changed. As you progress through each level, spotting these differences will only get harder and harder. Can you spot the difference?
